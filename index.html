Services

We offer a variety of different products, depending on the scope of your project, what media your final project is, or what specialties you may need.

2d illustration

Orthographic views of elevations, plans or all with light studies and shading information. We take your drawings, add color and texture to them. Great for pamphlets, and quick presentations. If you plan on going to 3D in a later stage, or plan on making architecture changes, let us know and we can make a massing model to easily adjust positions of walls, color changes, fixture types, and other changes.

3d illustration

A full 3D model of your building or other object with textures, scale items, shading and lighting information, with a number of different views. Great for presentations where scale is an important factor, and for getting a good idea of what the building will look like in real life around its surroundings. We can also composite your building into an existing photo, for an even more accurate depiction of the context of your project.

Animation

A short movie of your building with moving camera, to get a full experience of what if would feel like to move through your building and see views you might not see in stills. It's also a great way to see site plans and walkway flow around the proposed project area, we can add people, cars, trucks, and even boats if you're on the coast.

Interactive walkthrough

A fully immersive controllable walkthrough of your proposed project, where you decide where to walk to, where to look to, and how the building feels to walk around in. Almost like a video game, so you can pick out what things you want to see, get a feel for how big the bedroom really feels.
